Get to know us

"We are Gino and Daniele, the two chefs of Gambero Rosso. We've been friends since we know each other and it has always been about eating.

No weekend in our youth without experimenting while our parents and grannies had been to church. We'll never forget the lectures about not using our grandma's best pan or not turning on the oven without adults in the house.

We've always been curious about food and this curiosity has been tying us togheter so strongly, that we founded the Gambero Rosso 18 years ago.

We'd like to share this curiosity and courage to taste different things that you've known. Follow us on a new path, and you will (re)discover a joy of eating you've never known before."
