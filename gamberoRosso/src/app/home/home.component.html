Why choose us

There are many restaurants out there, what makes us unique?

  1. Unite gourmet approach with tradition

    Our plates are not a clean and formal arrangement of foods, nor an overloaded, well intended portion by an italian granny. We only use high quality ingredients produced in the region by people we know, trying to conserve their original taste throughout the cooking process and not forgetting about the little "secrets" of traditional italian cuisine.

  2. Unique atmosphere

    We position ourselves in the middle of androgyn style, freezing gourmet temples and crowded, popular restaurants. Our wish is to create a cosy atmosphere without overstepping the limits of other customers well-being.

  3. We lend an ear to customers with special needs

    Why should our cuisine be restricted to some people? Do you need a gluten-free menu or are you suffering from other food-allergies like tree nuts, cow's milk or wheat. Just let us know, we can manage that!
